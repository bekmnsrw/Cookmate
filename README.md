# Cookmate
**Cookmate** - app providing recipes from different categories

***

The first screen displays list of food categories: beef, lamb and chicken dishes, desserts, etc

  <img src="https://github.com/bekmnsrw/Cookmate/assets/90272601/6776f143-14ec-48f5-861c-38651edf3e50" height="520" width="240">
</p>

***

When you click on category's card, you can navigate to screen displaying list of dishes related to this category 

<p align="center">
  <img src="https://github.com/bekmnsrw/Cookmate/assets/90272601/d5b399e9-43b3-4862-8ea4-2705bd581d3e" height="520" width="240">
</p>

***

To see the recipe you need to click on button "Recipe"

<p align="center">
  <img src="https://github.com/bekmnsrw/Cookmate/assets/90272601/0320a1d3-341a-4bf4-ad4f-cfe265cb1e49" height="520" width="240">
</p>

***

Bottom navigation allows you to navigate to settings screen, where you can change app appereance: theme (light or dark), color accent and font size
